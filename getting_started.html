Introduction

This page will help you understand what each module will teach and where you want to start.

The Four Modules

  1. Leadership Within

    The characteristics, skills, and knowledge within yourself that is needed to be a good leader.

  2. Leadership Without

    The actions and outward skills that are needed to be a good leader.

  3. Learning How To Learn

    Skills and knowledge that helps people improve their learning capabilities.

  4. Personal Finance

    The ideas necessary to understanding the importance of personal finance and the skills that are needed to be capable.
