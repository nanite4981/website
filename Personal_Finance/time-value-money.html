Time Value of Money

We don't translate our money to time. We focus on money as a whole. We have an annual income, and we use that.

Related Objective

Money can be directly translated into how long it took us to get that money. Use this to help you understand the costs of what you want.

Money has a value in the present value (PV) and a value in the future (FV).

FV = PV + PV * (1 + interest rate / times interest was applied per period)^(time periods elapsed * times interest was applied per period)

There's a lot of equations you can use to calculate value. This is a simple one for compound interest.

Know the value of your money and don't discount the effects of time.
