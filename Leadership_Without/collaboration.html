Delegation and Collaboration

Teamwork is the name of the game. You have to work together, and a leader's job is to facilitate that.

Delegation

You need to learn your team's capabilities so that you can delegate tasks that fit each individual and lets them work the most effectively.

Collaboration

Co-create in your mind first. Build the idea of what you are looking for. Get input and create a greater product.

Then, you co-create the physical product. You each help each other and create a greater product.
