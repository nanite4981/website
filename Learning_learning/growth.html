Growth

There's a difference between learning and growing. You don't want to be limited to knowing things intellectually. It doesn't help to know what a Gantt chart does if you don't understand it, understand how you would want to use it, and how to assess its effects.

Related Objective

Learning will help you more if you change with it. Learning is just a tool to help you grow.

Start with the experts. The people who really understand things are often capable of bringing that understanding to you.
