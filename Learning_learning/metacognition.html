Metacognition

Your brain has a couple modes: focus mode and diffuse mode.

Related Objective

When your brain is in focus mode, it's using existing neural pathways to accomplish something.

When your brain is in diffuse mode, it's more relaxed. The brain has time to process. It may be slower, but it helps you form new neural pathways instead of using existing pathways to try to understand what you're learning.

You need to understand yourself and how you think. Once you know that, you will be able to improve those parts of yourself that help you learn and grow.

Prime your brain through preparation, give it time to relax, and learn in a way that brings you joy.
